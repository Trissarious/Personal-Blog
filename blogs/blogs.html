New HBO show, “Euphoria,” shines a stark light on adolescent issues

There’s a new teen drama on HBO that parents will want to know about. Euphoria follows "a group of high school students as they navigate drugs, sex, identity, trauma, social media, love and friendship." Euphoria (based on an Israeli mini-series of the same name) premiered on HBO June 16th, and releases new episodes on Sunday nights.

Denis Villeneuve’s Dune Is A Future-Shock Masterpiece

Even as someone who writes about movies for a living, I have to admit that I was in no rush to return to a brick-and-mortar theater. It had been 18 months since I’d last seen a film on the big screen. But sometimes exceptions need to be made, which is how I found myself sitting down (rather nervously and double-masked) in the too-packed confines of Alice Tully Hall for the New York Film Festival’s premiere of Dune.

Markiplier Directing & Starring in Movie Adaptation of Indie Horror Game Iron Lung

Markiplier’s upcoming film adaptation of the horror indie game ‘Iron Lung’ receives its first official trailer on Oct 16, 2023. It reached one million views within 24 hours, with thousands excited for what’s to come. News of this movie began spreading five months ago when the first teaser was released.
